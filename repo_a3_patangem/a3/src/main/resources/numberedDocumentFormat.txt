A Numbered Document consists of a sequence
of lines, each starts with a consecutive integer N followed by a :
and then has the lines content.

EXAMPLE BEGIN
1: This is the first line
2: This is the second line
3: some more content
4: Fire engines are red
EXAMPLE END

The content of line 1 is "This is the first line".
The content of line 2 is "This is the second line".
The content of line 4 is "Fire engines are red".
